Welcome to InterVarsity Christian Fellowship at FGCU.

Fly Higher, our weekly fellowship meeting, kicks off Friday, January 20th

We are located at Calvary Chapel, 20991 Three Oaks parkway, Estero, FL, 33928.

Come to Fly Higher to:
  • Experience the power of prayer with other Believers
  • Journey deep into Bible passage truths together
  • Enjoy fellowship with a coffee bar, muscial and spoken performances (perhaps your own) and board/card games with friends
Prayer is at 6:30 p.m. The Bible study starts at 7:30 p.m. and is followed by the fellowship/Coffee House at 8:30 p.m.

If you want more information, send an email to Andrew at adowen5643@eagle.fgcu.edu.
